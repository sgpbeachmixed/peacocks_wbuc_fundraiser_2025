Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How does this fundraiser work?
Donors can select from 2 types of baseline runs.

For $2, 1 baseline run is added to the team's total. These runs will be distributed evenly among the team.
For $5, you can assign 1 baseline run to a specific player on the team.
How do I contribute?
1. Click on the yellow button to start
2. Enter quantity of baseline runs
3. You will be redirected to a Google form to transfer $ and upload payment proof
How long will this fundraiser go on for?
Till mid-October, so don't miss this golden opportunity to get back at one of us!
More examples
A donates $2 → 1 baseline, split across the entire team.

B donates $10 → 2 assigned baselines
If assigned target not specified, it will go to the team aka 5 baselines.

C donates $100 → 20 assigned baselines / 10 assigned + 25 team baselines etc.
What is considered 1 baseline run?
1 baseline = length of 1 beach ultimate field (end line to end line), 75m according to WFDF
How do I view the sprints leaderboard?
The leaderboard will be continuously updated on our Instagram @sgp.beachmixed - follow to stay updated!
When will the baselines be run?
We will collate the number of sprints to complete each week. Our players will run their baselines at training.

What will my contributions go toward?
The bulk of our expenses for this campaign is for tournament-related fees, accommodation, official tournament kit, transportation and physio.

This fundraiser's aim is to ease our team physio cost. Any excess amount will go toward subsidising our team + player fee.
